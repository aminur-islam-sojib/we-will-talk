# Our Promise

This is my timer for us.

- I made this because we promised to talk again in 12 months.
- It counts down to the day we said we would reconnect.
- It shows the days, hours, minutes, seconds, and milliseconds until that moment.

## Why I made this

I wanted something to remind me of our promise and how much I’m looking forward to talking with you again.
This is not a repeating timer. It is a fixed countdown to the day we chose:

- **Target date:** `June 27, 2027`
- **Target time:** `00:00:00`

Every time I open this page, it shows how much time is left until we meet again.

## My message to you

I made this for us with love. Every second on the timer is a reminder that I am waiting for that day and thinking of you.

> "I’m counting down to the moment we talk again."
